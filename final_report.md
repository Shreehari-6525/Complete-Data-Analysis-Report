# Complete-Data-Analysis-Report

# E-commerce Sales Analysis Report

## Objective
The objective of this project is to perform a complete data analysis on e-commerce sales data to identify sales trends, product performance, and regional contribution using data visualization techniques.

---

## Dataset Overview
The dataset contains sales transaction records with the following attributes:
- Date
- Product
- Quantity
- Price
- Customer ID
- Region
- Total Sales

The data was cleaned and the Date column was converted into a proper datetime format for time-based analysis.

---

## Key Analysis Performed
- Calculated total revenue generated.
- Analyzed sales performance by product.
- Studied monthly sales trends.
- Evaluated sales contribution by region.

---

## Visual Insights

### 1. Sales by Product (Bar Chart)
- The bar chart shows a comparison of total sales across different products.
- Laptops generated the highest revenue, followed by Tablets and Phones.
- Headphones and Monitors contributed relatively lower sales.

### 2. Monthly Sales Trend (Line Chart)
- The line chart represents sales performance over time.
- Sales peaked in March, indicating strong demand during that period.
- A significant drop in sales is observed in April, suggesting possible seasonal or market factors.

### 3. Sales Distribution by Region (Pie Chart)
- The North and South regions together contribute more than 60% of total sales.
- The East and West regions show comparatively lower contribution.
- This insight can help businesses focus marketing and inventory strategies region-wise.

---

## Key Findings
- Product category plays a major role in revenue generation, with electronics performing the best.
- Sales are not consistent month-to-month, highlighting the importance of trend analysis.
- Regional performance varies significantly and should be considered in business planning.

---

## Conclusion
This project demonstrates a complete data analysis workflow including data loading, cleaning, analysis, and visualization. By using appropriate chart types such as bar charts, line charts, and pie charts, meaningful business insights were derived. These insights can help stakeholders make informed decisions related to sales strategy, inventory management, and regional focus.
